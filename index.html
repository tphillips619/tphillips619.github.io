Using Public Data to Visualize Changes in Home Prices

Visualizing trends in home prices over time and by geographic area. The first chart displays the home prices specific to Zip Code 92610 displayed in the context of similar homes in the county that it belongs to (Orange County.) The second chart displays the average 30-year fixed interest rate mortgage and the average points paid at that interest rate. The third chart uses the year over year forecast for the change in home prices for each city in Orange County.
